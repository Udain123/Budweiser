Budweiser X Sergio Ramos- Budweiser, has launched a new partnership with Sergio Ramos, current captain of Real Madrid and the Spanish national football team, as part of its ‘Beer of Kings’ campaign. By purchasing these special-edition bottles fans can unlock exclusive content which offers a deeper perspective, in Ramos’ own words, into the meaning of each of the tattoos. The limited-edition bottles will be available through e-commerce and at participating convenience retailers across the UK. Ramos’ ‘Beer of Kings’ campaign celebrates the star’s stand-out career in advance of his latest milestone, the soon-to-be most capped player ever for Spain .Budweiser’s football portfolio has continued to grow with the recent signing of multi-year partnerships with two of the top international football leagues: the Premier League and La Liga.
